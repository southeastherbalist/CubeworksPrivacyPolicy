Privacy Policy for Cube Word

Effective date: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Cube Word ("the App", "we", "us"), published by Loafworks, handles information when you play. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game and its optional online features.

  • Display name. If you choose to play the Daily Challenge and submit a score, the display name you enter is sent to and stored on our server so it can appear on the public leaderboard. Entering a name is optional; if you leave it blank, scores are submitted as "Anonymous".
  • Gameplay data. When you submit a Daily Challenge score we store your score, the time you took, and the words you formed, alongside the challenge date, to build the leaderboard.
  • Advertising identifiers and related data. The App shows ads using Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect your device's advertising ID and related device information to serve and measure ads. See Google's policies below.
  • On-device data. Game settings, statistics, and your local game history are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us.

2. How we use information

  • To operate the Daily Challenge and display public leaderboards.
  • To show and measure advertising (via Google AdMob).
  • To process the optional "Remove Ads" purchase (via Google Play Billing).

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Advertising is provided by Google AdMob, which acts as a third party and may use device identifiers for ad personalization, measurement, and fraud prevention. You can learn more and manage your choices here:

  • How Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • You can reset or delete your advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

Purchasing "Remove Ads" within the App stops these ads from being shown.

4. Purchases

The optional "Remove Ads" purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details; we only store a flag on your device indicating the purchase was made.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is processed by the service providers that power the App's online features: our backend hosting provider (Supabase) for the leaderboard, and Google (AdMob for ads, Google Play for purchases). Leaderboard entries (display name, score, time) are shown publicly to other players within the App.

6. Data retention and deletion

On-device data can be cleared at any time using "Delete Data" in the App's Options. To request deletion of leaderboard data associated with your display name from our server, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Security

Data transmitted to our server and to Google's services is sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
